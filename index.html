We are on the rise

The proportion of staff with PhD is currently at 21% (target is 60% in next 10 yrs)

  1. • The per capita research publication output has increased to 0.45, closer to Inter University Council for East Africa norm of one publication per staff per year
  2. • Ranking : UR is now the second most influential university in East Africa, in terms of the impact of its publications, as measured by the Normalized Citation Impact (Clarivate Analytics, 2016).
  3. • The positioning of UR in the Web of Universities Rankings has improved to being in the Top 100 in Africa.

Three Centres have a continental mandate and are funded by the World Bank as African Centres of Excellence (ACEs) :

  • ACE in the Internet of Things
  • ACE for Data Science
  • IoT
